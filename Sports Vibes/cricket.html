About cricket
Cricket is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each.
It is a bat-and ball game played on a roughly oval grass feild
in the centre of which is a flat strip of ground 20.12m (22 yards)long, called a pitch.
At each end of the pitch is a set of wooden stumps, called a wicket.
Cricket can be played for competition or for fun.
Cricket is a good sport for developing overall fitness, stamina and hand-eye coordination.
Cricket uses a hard ball, so protective gear should be
worn to avoid injury.
